## 2. Market Benchmark Reference

Per the **Logistics Tech Vendor Survey 2025 (LTVS-2025, §4.2, p.34)**, ruggedized handheld scanners with mid-range specs (1D/2D imager, IP65, Wi-Fi 6) traded at a median price of **$562/unit at 500+ unit quantities** in Q4 2025. Bottom-quartile pricing reached **$528/unit** for buyers with multi-year service commitments.

## 3. Payment Terms Policy

Per Meridian Finance Policy §7.3 (Vendor Payment Terms):

- **Preferred:** Net-60 from invoice date.
- **Acceptable without escalation:** Net-45, provided the vendor offers a concession of equivalent value (e.g., extended warranty at no charge, expedited delivery, or price reduction ≥ 1%).
- **Avoid:** Net-30 unless offsetting price reduction makes total cost-of-capital favorable.

## 4. Delivery Requirements

Hard on-site deadline: **August 30, 2026**. Warehouse 4 go-live is September 5, 2026; scanners must be commissioned and operator-trained in the intervening 5 business days. **Any delivery date later than August 30 is grounds for walk-away.**

## 5. Walk-Away Criteria

The negotiation should terminate (WALK_AWAY) if any of the following hold after best-effort negotiation:

1. Unit price exceeds $580 with no offsetting concessions.
2. Delivery commitment slips past August 30, 2026.
3. Warranty offered is less than 2 years.
4. Vendor requires payment terms shorter than Net-30 with no price compensation.

## 6. Fallback Option (Negotiation Leverage)

We hold an indicative alternate from **Honeywell-X40 via authorized reseller** at approx. $595/unit, but with 14-week lead time and stricter Net-30 terms — viable only as a fallback if primary negotiation fails. Strongly prefer single-source for warranty and support continuity.
